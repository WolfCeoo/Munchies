About Munchies Delivery Service

Founded by Andile Mathe and Kabelo Matseke, Munchies Delivery Service Pty Ltd is a hyper-local food delivery platform serving Soshanguve and surrounding areas. We connect residents with authentic township vendors for fast, reliable delivery of kota, pap & steak, chicken stew, mogodu, and more.
