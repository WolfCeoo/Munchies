Official Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Munchies Delivery Service (Pty) Ltd (CIPC Reg No: 2026/103543/07) values your privacy. We collect limited personal information to provide food delivery services in Soshanguve in strict accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

Data We Collect

  • ✓ Name and phone number for order placement and delivery updates
  • ✓ Delivery address for accurate service
  • ✓ WhatsApp messages for order communication

How We Use Your Data

Data is used only for processing orders, coordinating with vendors/drivers, and sending delivery updates via WhatsApp. Personal data is securely processed via Airtable and communication is managed through Meta (WhatsApp Business API). We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as required for delivery (e.g., vendor or driver).

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. WhatsApp uses end-to-end encryption for messages, and our internal records are protected by industry-standard security protocols.

Your Rights

In line with POPIA, you may contact us at munchies.ds@gmail.com to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time.
