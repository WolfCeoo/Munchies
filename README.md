# Munchies Delivery Service - Official Website

![Munchies Logo](assets/munchies-logo.jpg)

## Overview
Munchies Delivery Service Pty Ltd (Reg: 2026/103543/07) is a hyper-local food delivery platform based in **Soshanguve, Pretoria**. We connect local residents with authentic township vendors, providing fast and reliable delivery via WhatsApp.

## Features
- **Live Menus:** Integrated with Airtable to provide real-time updates from vendors.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Seamless ordering flow directly to our dispatch team.
- **Soshanguve Focused:** Specialized delivery service for Blocks A-XX and surrounding areas.
- **Compliance Ready:** Full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service included for Meta Business Verification.
